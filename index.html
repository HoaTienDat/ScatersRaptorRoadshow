OUR DNA

Scaters does not just manufacture skateboards; Scaters engineers the ride.

Scaters was founded in a Bristol garage with a simple mission: break the mold. While others focused on graphics, Scaters focused on physics and material science.

We believe in supporting local skate shops because they are the heart of the community. That's why you won't find an "Add to Cart" button here. Scaters drives traffic to the people who keep the scene alive.
